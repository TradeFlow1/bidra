# Bidra V2 launch readiness

## Core launch rule

A successful launch means a buyer can browse, Buy Now, schedule pickup, show up, complete the handover, and leave feedback with minimal friction.

## Required before launch

- Listings use structured fields, not loose free text for critical data.
- Pickup availability is required before a listing can go live.
- Buy Now creates a binding sale state.
- Pickup scheduling is mandatory.
- Reschedules happen through structured in-app flow.
- No-shows and repeated late changes are tracked.
- Reliability consequences can be applied by the platform.
- Messages do not override order state or schedule rules.
- Completion happens after real handover.
- Feedback follows completion.

## Launch scope

- Household categories only.
- Narrow launch geography.
- Dense listing quality over broad category coverage.
- Trust and completion rate matter more than raw listing count.

## Metrics that matter

- Buy Now to scheduled pickup rate
- Scheduled pickup to completed handover rate
- No-show rate
- Late reschedule rate
- Repeat offender rate

## Non-negotiables

- No escrow
- No deposits
- No in-app payments
- No auctions
- No counter-offers
- Messaging is support-only and cannot override schedule
